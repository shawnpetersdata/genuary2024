Genuary 4 - Pixels

I really wanted to avoid pixel art with this prompt, so I looked at how LCD TVs display images and interpreted that as pixels.

Artworks were scaled down to 64x64 and displayed based on the brightness of red, green, and blue values.

The images were coming out a little dark, so I layered in a little backlight based on the intensity of the colors. As far as I know, this is a problem for many TV types, but a solution here.
