Genuary 29 - Shooting Stars

The mathematics behind signed distance functions (SDFs) intrigues me, but trying to implement them in p5.js last year left me unimpressed by their speed.

Since shaders seem optimized for the repetitive pixel by pixel calculations that SDFs are built on I decided to try that route. I've fairly beginner level when it comes to shaders, but I'm glad to say that I managed to implement many stars being animated being drawn solely based on SDF calculations.
