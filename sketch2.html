Genuary 2 - No Palettes

My biggest concern in working without palettes is ensuring that the contrast is high enough to see the piece.

I typically use RGB colours, but HSB (hue,saturation,brightness) seemed to make more sense here.

I limited the range of saturation and brightness to give a range that I preferred and then randomly chose a starting hue for the background.

For stroke colors, I used the complement of the background and an additional color adjacent to the stroke color.

For the actual piece, I used noise generated over 50 frames to generate 2 main jagged lines in opposition, getting progressively noiser from left to right.
