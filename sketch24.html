Genuary 24 - Irregular Penrose Triangles

I found that someone had sketched out a penrose triangle using vertices, so I repurposed it to make my own. I used HSL values to keep the colouring the same while applying darkness and lightness to give the required appearance.

Once I was happy with the overall look, I tiled it on an irregular rectangular grid and varied the colouring based on noise. Simple looking piece, but it actually took me longer than some of the more complex ones.
