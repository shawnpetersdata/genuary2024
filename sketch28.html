Genuary 28 - Found Haiku

Skeuomorphism refers to qualities of objects that are derived from earlier versions yet serve no purpose in modern versions. In this piece I included a typerwriter style font on a paper texture with typewriting animation and flickering candlelight; none of which are necessary for a generative poetry project.

To fit the overall scene, I used rita.js and regular expressions to search through The Adventures of Sherlock Holmes by Arthur Conan Doyle for naturally occurring haikus. There is some issue when the text contains numbers and abbreviations, but the result is typically in the correct format.
