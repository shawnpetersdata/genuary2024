Genuary 1 - Particles (Lots of them)

I wanted to create something interactive for this sketch so I came up with particles being influenced by movement.

To keep the framerate reasonable, I limited the total amount of particles and used a circle packing algorithm to arrange them.

When movement is detected, the particles move, and when there is no motion, they try to restore the original position.

The particles update their color based on the webcam information of where they are meant to be located.
