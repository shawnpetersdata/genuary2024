Genuary 18 - Bauhaus Circles

I focused on the minimalistic and geometric themes that seem to be a major piece of Bauhaus artwork.

I used a lot of randomness to add in some variation which I think really brings something special to some of the generated pieces.

As I was putting this together all of the circles and semi-circles reminded me of the CBC logo, so instead of using primary colours, I took inspiration from their 1970s - 1980s logo.
