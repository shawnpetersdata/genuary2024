Genuary 17 - Islamic Tiling

A neat effect, but not exactly what I was going for. This is definitely worth revisiting in the future.

I set up construction points based on a 4 fold setup and randomly chose which points would be connected. Since I am terrible at geometry, I limited my algorithm to create square patterns that I could easily tesselate.

I don't think I fully captured the complexity of Islamic geometric patterns, but it was definitely a learning experience.
